manager

MAN'AGER, n. One who has the conduct or direction of any thing; as the manager of a theater; the manager of a lottery, of a ball, &c.

A skilful manager of the rabble.

An artful manager, that crept between--

1. A person who conducts business with economy and frugality; a good husband.

A prince of great aspiring thoughts; in the main, a manager of his treasure.

unkindly

UNKINDLY, a.

1. Unnatural; contrary to nature; as an unkindly crime.

2. Unfavorably; malignant; as an unkindly fog.

UNKINDLY, adv.

1. Without kindness; without affection; as, to treat one unkindly.

2. In a manner contrary to nature; unnaturally.

All works of nature, abortive, monstrous, or unkindly mix'd.

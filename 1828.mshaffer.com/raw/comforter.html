comforter

COMFORTER, n.

1. One who administers comfort or consolation; one who strengthens and supports the mind in distress or danger.

I looked for comforters, but found none. Ps. 69.

Miserable comforters are ye all. Job. 16.

2. The title of the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in my name--he shall teach you all things. John 14.

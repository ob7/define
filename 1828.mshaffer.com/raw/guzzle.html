guzzle

GUZ'ZLE, v.i. To swallow liquor greedily; to drink much; to drink frequently.

Well seasoned bowls the gossip's spirits raise,

Who, while she guzzles, chats the Doctor's praise.

GUZ'ZLE. v.t. To swallow much or often; to swallow with immoderate gust.

--Still guzzling must of wine.

GUZ'ZLE, n. An insatiable thing or person.

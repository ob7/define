plebeian

PLEBE'IAN, a. [L. plebeius, from plebs, the common people.]

1. Pertaining to the common people; vulgar; as plebeian minds; plebeian sports.

2. Consisting of common people; as a plebeian throng.

PLEBE'IAN, n. One of the common people or lower ranks of men. [Usually applied to the common people of ancient Rome.]

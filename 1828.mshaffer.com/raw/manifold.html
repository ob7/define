manifold

MAN'IFOLD, a. [many and fold.] Of divers kinds; many in number; numerous; multiplied.

O Lord, how manifold are thy works! Ps.104.

I know your manifold transgressions. Amos.5.

1. Exhibited or appearing at divers times or in various ways; applied to words in the singular number; as the manifold wisdom of God, or his manifold grace. Eph.3. 1 Pet.4.

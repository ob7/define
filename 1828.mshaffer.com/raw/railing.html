railing

RA'ILING, ppr.

1. Clamoring with insulting language; uttering reproachful words.

2. a. Expressing reproach; insulting; as a railing accusation. 2Peter 2.

RA'ILING, n. Reproachful or insolent language. 1Peter 3.

RA'ILING, ppr. Inclosing with rails.

RA'ILING, n.

1. A series of rails; a fence.

2. Rails in general; or the scantling for rails.

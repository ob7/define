tender-hearted

TEN'DER-HE`ARTED, a. [tender and heart.]

1. Having great sensibility; susceptible of impressions or influence.

--When Rehoboam was young and tenderhearted, and could not withstand them. 2 Chron. 13.

2. Very susceptible of the softer passions of love, pity or kindness.

Be ye kind one to another, and tender-hearted. Eph.9.

monstrosity

MONSTROS'ITY, n. The state of being monstrous, or out of the common order of nature.

We often read of monstrous births; but we see a greater monstrosity in education, when a father begets a son and trains him up into a beast.

1. An unnatural production; that which is monstrous.

Fabri arranges distortions, gibbosities, tumors, &c. in the class of morbific monstrosities.

A monstrosity never changes the name or affects the immutability of a species.
